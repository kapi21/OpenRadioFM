Hardware Specifications

  • Hardware: Compatible with MT8163, ESSGO, and JUNSU V1 8581a units.
  • Resolution: Optimized for 1024x600 screens and higher.
  • Radio Chip: NXP TEF6686 with full RDS support.
  • Firmware: Android 8.1+. Works with or without root (better performance with root).
